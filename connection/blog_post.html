Pyaar, ISHQ, Mohabbat

Saimi hu’e sii sham, parinde ghar loat rhe the, naram dhoop mein lipta hua ek aangan, cricket ka ek thaka hua game, bachon ka shore, radio pe kishore, ek khamosh kutta, aur kisi ko pata nahi challa, ussi galii ki gumnan si chatt pe ek aadmii, zindagi aur moat k kaagar pe khara hua tha. Kehte hai aakhiri lamhon mein poori life flashback mein dikhti hai, usse toh bas ek wohi larki baar baar dikh rahi thi.
Pyaar karna boht log sikhate hai, par afsos uss pyaar ko bhulate kese hai, yeh koi nahi sikhata.
Kuch gaane aapki zindagi se yoon hi judh jaate hai... unki dhun mein lipte hote hai chote badhe kisse... kayi saari yaadein.
Ek lafz mein toh use sirf aafat e kaha ja sakta hai, atrangi thi thodi, pagal thi, baat baat pe mann badal leti thi, zindagi se behad pyaar tha usey or khud se zindagi se bhi zyada. 10 saal ki umar mein, usne kaha, mujhe tmara gahr dekhna hai, apne ghar le chalo... Mere chehre pr moochon ka nishan tak nahi aaya tha, half paint mein, balon mein tail laga kar ghoomte the.. Lekin mano aisa lag rha tha ki ek pal mein jawan hogye... jab ghar ponche toh us'se dekh kr mom ne kaha kis pari ko ghar laye ho, I said school mein saath parhte hai, kuch palon m us'ne sab ko apna bana liya tha..
Bachpan mein school k function mein, humne sath mein ek shaddi ka skit kia tha and we won the best skit ka award, it was so natural like we were meant for each other, har kisi ne yahii bola tha...
Badii ho’kar kia banogi is sawal ka roz alg alg jawab tha us k pass, har cheez aadhi adhuri chod deti film, kitaab, hobby.. Bhagwan ka ishara tha shayad mere liye par mujhe kahan fursat thi us’se se ki ishare dekh paun.
Kuch saalon baad usne school change kr diya laga shayad hamra safar itna hi tha, par school ki benches aur ghar ki chh’at toh milli thi, yeh safar kese khatam hota phr... Lekin agr ap koi nayi jaga jao toh naye dost mil jate hai aur purane ko ap bhool e jate ho...
Aur phr ek din college mein 23-August-2013 us chemistry ki class mein humne nomenclatures seekha, meri wali thunderstorm toh chup chaap dabe paao class mein enter hue ek boring se Friday ko ek naye uniform k saath. 500 sq.ft k canteen mein akele kone mein bethi thi, kaan mein headphone aur aankhon mein mere liye shaq... that look, oh God that look, finally bolii suno this will change your life, matlb kia tha? woh gaana, woh head phones, yaa phr woh khud.. mujhe toh aaj b lagta hai uski nazar srif samose par thi...
Uss din, Zindagii ek baar phr jud gayi, Kuch bhi tedaa..medhaa ulta seedha...ut-ptang karahi leti thi vo mujhse....vo gabbar main shamba...vo Munna..main circuit..vo guruji...main bhola.
Sab se bada din college farewell party 25-April-2015... party krte krte kuch naye words bhi jud gaye the vocabulary mein curshes, rebounds, hormones, boyfriend, girlfriend, good friend, just friend, its complicated etc etc.. Farewell toh khatam ho e gaya par pata nahi kyu aisa laga kuch or bhi ek baar phr khatam hogya tha uss din. Final score tha Life: 2, Anish: 0.
Jinnah bagh ki lights Ki tarah kuch Feelings On-Off On-Off Ho Rahi Thi.. Hum padosi toh abhi bhi the par achanak hamre stops phr se alg alg hogye the..
Pyar mein itne thokar khane ke bawajood, zakhm pe Iodex malkar veer jawaan hamesha khade ho jaate hai... ek aur try karne ke liye.
Kitna ajeeb hai na hum dono alg alg raaste gaye, alg alg shahar, alg alg careers, or 2 saal baad 24-December-2017 mein meri nazar us pari par ja’ponchi, raat k 10 bje Islamabad ki Gloria Jeans coffee shop pr coffee p rahi thi... university ki trip pr aayi hu’e thi...
Jab love ki spelling bhi nahi aati thi ... matlab toh door ki baat thi ... tab se jisse pyar kiya hai aur jab woh chod kar chali jaye... toh jaan bas nikalte nikalte reh jaati thi. Laakhon baar us pal ko rewind kar kar k play kia tha mene magical dim lighitng, beautiful dresses, background m piano jesi movies ya romatic novels mein hamresha hota hai. Par yeh, yeh toh meri love story thi.
2 saal baad jb mille, us ne kaha hum touch mein rahenge, aur woh Karachi wapis chali gayi. Usi raat uska facebook pr message aaya aur us din laga meri train wapis track par aagyai hai, 1 saal matlb 23-august-2018 tk hum baat krte rhe, har comma, har fullstop yaad hai mujhe aaj bhi, har lafz k peeche chupe emotions bhi mehsoos kiye mene, par us k baad pata nahi kia hua usne bat krna band kar diya... Gussa keh lo, Ego ya aur kuch, mene b phr koi message nahi kiya, shayad kehne sunine ko kuch baaki nahi raha tha. Chapter closed.
Phir ek din I decided to move on, 3-September-2019 mein meri degree complete hu’e, mujhe bht sare badhai wale phone calls aur messages bhi aane lage, Itefaq se ek din ek aur message bhi aaya. Us din Hi Hello k baad conversation shuru hote e khatam hogyi..
Phr 2 Saal baad 3-January-2021 ko, Instagram k message request wale section m ek message aata hai, “Kesse Ho? Pechana?”
15 saal se ek love story khatam karne ki koshish kar rha hu mein, century toh maar li, lekin match haar gaya tha...
Woh jis key bager mein kabhi poora nahii ho sakta, aadhi khatam classic milde se behtr, baarish mein half cutting chaaye se achi, ya Rishikesh Mukherjee ki Aanand k aakhiri 10 minutes se zyada heart breaking, sharabi dost, Zakir bhai ka tabla, Sachin Tendulkar ka straight drive, dhak dhak karti hu’e Madhuri, yaa tip tip barsaat mein Raveena, Shreya Ghosal ki aawaz, binaa kuch saman k woh sulhje alfaz, in sab se zyada mein pyaar krta tha us’se and I wanted her back, na koi gaana na background mein koi aawaz, na 16 peice orchestra srif mein, or meri tezz hoti hu’e dharkanein, jab pata chala she is getting married, dill k tuqre tuqre hogye us din, aur har ek Arjit Singh k sad song ki lyrics ka matlb samjh aaya mujhe us din.
Yeh meri kahaani thi, uski thodi alg hoti, lekin kia farq parta hai ab, usko uska Happy Ending toh milla.
Zindagi side 'A' se side 'B' ki tarah ghoomti gaano ki reel ki tarah hi toh hai ... kabhi koi gaana itna pasand hai ki khatam hone se dar lagta hai ... kabhi koi gaana poore din honthon se jaane ka naam nahi leta ... aur kabhi koi gaane ke sirf dhun yaad reh jaati hai.
